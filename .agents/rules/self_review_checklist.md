---
name: self_review_checklist
description: Forces a rigorous self review before concluding tasks.
---
Before you conclude any coding task or architectural design, you must perform a silent self review against the following criteria:
1. Are all inputs securely sanitized?
2. Are errors logged gracefully without exposing sensitive stack traces?
3. Are database queries optimized and indexed?
4. Does the architecture scale efficiently?
Do not finalize your output until all criteria are met.
